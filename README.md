# AI Lead Assistant

An AI-powered lead qualification and customer engagement system built with Laravel. It helps businesses capture leads, understand customer requirements, qualify prospects, and streamline sales workflows using AI.

---

## 🚀 Overview

AI Lead Assistant is designed to automate the early stages of the sales process.

Instead of manually responding to every inquiry, the system uses AI to:

- Collect customer information
- Understand customer requirements
- Qualify leads
- Score leads
- Store leads in CRM
- Notify the sales team
